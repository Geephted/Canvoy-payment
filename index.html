Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I create an account on your platform?

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of our homepage. You'll need to provide your name, email address, and create a password. We'll send a verification email to confirm your account.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Verve and MasterCard. Additionally, you can transfer directly from your bank app. All transactions are securely processed with bank-level encryption.

Can I change or cancel my subscription plan?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences in pricing. Cancelled subscriptions remain active until the end of your current billing period.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link. For security, this link expires after 1 hour. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.

What kind of customer support do you offer?

We offer 24/7 customer support through multiple channels. You can reach us via live chat, email, or phone. Additionally, we have an extensive knowledge base with tutorials and documentation to help you find answers quickly.
